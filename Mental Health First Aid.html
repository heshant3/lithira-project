Overview

We understand that mental health is the precondition of the whole wellbeing at Wellnest Solutions. Our Mental Health First Aid scheme empowers the citizens with knowledge of how to offer first aid to a person with mental health problems. Regardless of whether it is the stress, anxiety, or the moment of crisis, trained volunteers can change the life of the troubled individual with their empathy, advice, and contact with the professional resources. With this program, you are joining one of the caring communities whose goal is to shatter the stigma associated with mental health and promote the culture of caring and empathy.
