Location: Community Center Colombo / Mobile Clinics

Overview

Our Health Screening Assistant program equips communities with the tools to identify early signs of physical health risks, from hypertension to diabetes. Through free screenings, educational workshops, and personalized follow-ups, we empower individuals to take proactive control of their wellbeing. Join us in bridging the gap between prevention and care
